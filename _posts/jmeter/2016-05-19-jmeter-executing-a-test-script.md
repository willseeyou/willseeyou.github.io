### JMeter测试脚本有两种运行方式
1. GUI Mode:  `/Users/will/apache-jmeter-3.0/bin/jmeter`
2. Non-GUI Mode:  `/Users/will/apache-jmeter-3.0/bin/jmeter -n -t [path to test script] -l [path to results files]`
